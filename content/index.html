The event will take place from June 22–26, 2026, and it is hosted at the Institute of Mathematics at LMU Munich. The conference is part of the series of annual meetings and conferences of the AofA community.

Flajolet Lecture

The Flajolet Lecture is given by the recipient of the Philippe Flajolet Lecture Prize 2025, mathematician and computer scientist extraordinaire,

Hsien-Kuei Hwang (Institute of Statistical Science, Academia Sinica, Taipei, Taiwan)

The Flajolet Lecture Prize is awarded for outstanding contributions to analytic combinatorics and analysis of algorithms, in memory of Philippe Flajolet.
